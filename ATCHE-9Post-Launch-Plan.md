## Visual Rhythm — Grid Consistency

Full version lives in the "Visual Rhythm" addendum at the end of `ATCHE-Instagram-Audit-StyleGuide.docx`. The short version:

- **Real photos** keep the existing warm, low-key, jewel-tone grade — documentary, never the gold-graphic treatment.
- **Branded graphics** (Posts 1 & 3, and future ones like them) follow one recipe: black ground, gold serif ATCHÉ wordmark, "(ah-SHAY)" pronunciation guide beneath it in cream italic, a thin gold rule under the wordmark, and a soft jewel-tone glow in one corner. That combination is the recurring "this is an ATCHÉ graphic" signal.
- **Captions**: brand-intro/major-announcement posts can open with "ATCHÉ (ah-SHAY) —"; real photo posts stay in first-person community voice, no tag.
- Alternate the two tracks in the posting order rather than clustering — real moments punctuated by branded statements.
