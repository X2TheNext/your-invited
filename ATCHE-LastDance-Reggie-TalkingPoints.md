## Format: Block Party

- Outdoor element — live music, activations spilling onto the street, not just inside the venue
- Built around the cluster of U Street businesses within walking distance — turns one event into a corridor activation
- Positions ATCHÉ as the connective tissue between venues/brands that don't normally cross-promote
