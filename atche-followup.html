How It Works

Your guide to the follow-up queue ✦

What you're looking at
Everyone who needs a follow-up — both models (people applying to ATCHÉ Circle) and venues (clubs and spaces we're working with). Sorted by who's most overdue first.
The workflow
① Tap a card to see the person
② Hit Copy next to IG DM or Text — message is now in your clipboard
③ Open Instagram or your texts, paste, send
④ Come back and tap ✓ Sent — pick when to follow up next
⑤ Card disappears from the queue ✦
The +3d button
Not the right moment? Hit +3d to snooze the card for 3 days. It'll come back when it's time.
The colors
Red border — overdue, send today
Gold border — due today
Green border — coming up soon
Don't edit the messages
The templates are written for you — they sound natural and match where each person is in the process. You can add a sentence at the end if you want, but the core message is good to go.
Want the full picture?
Read The Playbook — it explains what ATCHÉ is building, who each person in your queue is, and why your follow-ups matter so much to the business.
