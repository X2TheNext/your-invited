Questions, answered

Everything you’re wondering.

Is it paid? +
Yes — there’s a $50 appearance fee for this shoot, plus all the professional content you keep. This first night is a proof-of-concept, so think of the fee as the floor: the real money comes through the paid brand and venue bookings you unlock as part of The Circle. We pay our women — always.
Do I keep the photos? +
Yes. You get edited photos and video of yourself to post and keep. ATCHÉ also uses select content to feature you and promote the work.
How long will it take? +
Plan for about 2–3 hours in the evening. We’ll send the exact call time once you’re confirmed.
What do I wear? +
Chic and elevated, in your own style — bring 1 or 2 options. If you want direction, we’ll help you choose so everyone’s cohesive on camera.
Do I need modeling experience? +
Nope. We’re looking for confident, photogenic women with good energy. We’ll direct you on the night.
Is there a contract? +
Yes — a simple, clear release so everyone knows how the content is used. Nothing sketchy, and you’ll see it before you sign.
Who’s behind ATCHÉ? +
ATCHÉ is a community built for creative women in nightlife — to get booked, paid on time, and protected. DM @atcheofficial anytime with questions.
